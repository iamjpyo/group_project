What is VMS?

JB DigiWorks is an innovative digital app for both iOS and Android that lets you recreate the best moments in life either on still photos or video. DigiWorks has many useful features to enhance the photos and videos with editing tools, filters and effects. And when you are done, you can easily share your result with all of your friends and the rest of the world through Instagram and Facebook.

JB DigiWorks has been designed from the bottom up by photography experts and award-winning app developers to makesure you get the best digital app ever created. We are continuously improving the app with new features - this is just the beginning.
